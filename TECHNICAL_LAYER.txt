- Data: FBLA/lib/authStorage.ts (AsyncStorage-backed user DB + current session key).

- Redirect allowlist when unauthenticated: /landing, /auth-choice, /login, /signup.
- Profile completeness gate: forces /profile-setup until profileComplete is true.
